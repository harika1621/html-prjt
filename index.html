About  IF

Our IF was founded in 2020 during the Covid High Time. As there is high demand for food deliveries as everyone is staying in Quarentime. We decided to open this Indian Foods Store in order serve all people and fill their tummy's with our delicious Inian foods.

Do you know since when Biryani is in india?

According to historian Lizzie Collingham, the modern biryani developed in the royal kitchens of the Mughal Empire from 1526 to 1857 and is a mix of the native spicy rice dishes of India and the Persian pilaf. (Source: Wikipedia )
